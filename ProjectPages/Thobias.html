Made by me and my friend as a personal project, during college. I designed the whole combat system, movesets and character. Thobias the train fighter is an arena fighter with trains. Show them who is the most handsome ! use your extended moveset (40, maybe useful moves) and your insane speed to slaughter your enemies to death in this chaotic fighter.

Some Concepts arts

This project was a great kickstart for me to really improve at indie game development (one of the tools made for the combat system is still used in my current project EGO for example). As this game didn't amount to much, I'm still proud of what was done, because the basis for a real 3D fighting game is there.
