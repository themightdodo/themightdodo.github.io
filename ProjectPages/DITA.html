In an internship at Miroring, a small startup based in Montreal, I was asked to help them develop the game. I had to work on the shaders, the dialog and save system, and a bit of gameplay. I also helped at the backend part of the steam page.

Some visuals

In Death In The Afternoon, throw yourself into a night of drinking. You play as Arthur, a young student who likes to drink a little too much... Try to fight your addiction ! And choose your drinks and interactions carefully, or you might have the worst BlackOut of your life !

As the tone of the game revolved mainly around the alcohol theme, I made something very distorted, and liquid. Also the eyes are a means to judge you and laugh as you drink and fall deeper into the addiction.
