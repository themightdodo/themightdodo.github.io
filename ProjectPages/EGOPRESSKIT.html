About

A Smoke based 3D Beat’em all. Fight thousands of robots at a time through open-ended levels in this Arcade style Smoking Wizard game ! Dash, Jump, Climb, ignite your smoke and obliterate every bots in your way!

What's unique about EGO ?

A smoke based beat'em all

The particle based attacks gives a new twist about how we explode robots in games

Hack'n Smoke through an army

Reminiscent of the musou style of games, you fight hundreds of enemies at a time !

Dash through open-ended levels

As movement and smoke fight mechanics mix, find the most efficient way to the Bus! Speedrun an array of open-ended levels and become the best smoking wizard!  

Gigantic bossfights

After destroying a whole industry line of robots, EGO lets you destroy unique and gigantic robots!
