About

A Smoke based 3D Beat’em all. Fight thousands of robots at a time through open-ended levels in this Arcade style Smoking Wizard game ! Dash, Jump, Climb, ignite your smoke and obliterate every bots in your way!

What's unique about EGO ?

A new twist on the 3D action platformer genre

- The way you move through the levels and how you attack are codependent

- Levels are semi-open, you’ll need to learn and search the most efficient path to get the best score

No meta progression

- For a full arcade experience, the way you progress through the game is skill-based. The online leaderboard is coming soon in the free demo !

- The game is designed to be done in one sitting

About the dodging system

- It is not an I-frame dodge, and is also intended to be used as a running mechanic through the dodge extend (an orange circle appears after you jump, dodge at the right time to perform the dodge extend)

- Enables you to climb walls and fly

Organic adaptative difficulty through the transcendence system

- The transcendence (“transcendance” bar in the game) when is refilling, heals you

- Killing enemies drains your transcendence

- When the transcendence is draining the character is weakened

Time-based and efficiency based scoring system

- Killing a lot of enemies fast gives you more score points, to reward efficiency.

- Getting to the end of the level fast gives you more point also

- You can’t have access to the bus unless you have killed a quota of enemies, the levels are designed in a way that no backtracking is required

- Some levels have no enemies in them, so the only objective is to get to the end fast.

A physics based beat’em all

- Your attacks are literally smoke particles that you’ll need to apply to enemies

- Player and enemies movements are also completely prone to physics

Non euclidean levels

- There are non euclidean levels, and some levels with a mix of standard and non euclidean, to make traversal more interesting

A game without words

- there will be no text in the game, information will only be shown through iconography and drawings

About Boss fights

- The bosses are giant robots, with weak points represented as red lights, if you hit them good you’ll kill them fast
