It started as a fith semester solo school project, then was retaken this summer by me and my friend. I did everything but the enemy AI. Ego is a Hack'n slash in a top down view where you play as an elderly person revolting alone against the institution with the help of his smoke. Carry out sequences of attacks while smoking, set the clouds you have created on fire and explode those damn robots.

The particle system as a Game

The goal here was to create a game where particles would be launched as attacks by the player. To answer this problem, I decided to base this combat system on unity’s particle system coupled with an algorithm that determines how to englobe the particles with bounds. I optimized this system heavily with the unity job/burst system to utilize multi threading.

A challenge of visibility

This is still an ongoing challenge, as you may have seen from the video above, sometimes all of the interacting gameplay elements can get quite messy visually. Since the first version a lot has been done, from tweaking the tone mapping/saturation to using the design standard’s colors to our advantage. Currently the next step would be to create a shader to visually make the smoke less dominant on the screen.

A skill based game design

As all good arcade style games, this one is meant to be replayed a few times before you can fully grasp the deep mechanics. To represent this philosophy, here all of the character’s skills are available right from the start, and is inspired by the first Wizard of legend, and Noita. And the level design here is a mix of sonic the hedgehog and mario world inspirations, as a means for the player to learn the best routes to kill all of the enemies as fast as possible.
